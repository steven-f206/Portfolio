About Me

My name is Steven Ferreira. I'm a Brazilian American and very proud of my heritiage. I attended Gwinnett Technical College along with my wife, and intended to get a degree in computer science, however neither of us were making enough money to support each other and school, so I dropped out and got a job working at a car dealership. It paid the bills and then some, but it never gave me the career fulfilment I always wanted. It wasn't until six years later that i heard about GT's coding bootcamp. I decided to enroll, and the rest is history.

I love computers and, more specifically, I love to code.

Knowing that I will be able to help shape the technological world is amazing! I can only hope I will make a big enough impact on the world and make my sons proud.
